Imagine visualizing your musical journey as a subway map – every artist a station, every album a connection, and every song a stop along the way.

## What is Soundline?

Soundline transforms your Spotify listening history into stylish subway signs, inspired by iconic transit systems around the world. This project is part of my coursework at RH Köln.
